Things to remember

These requirements may change from time to time. We’ll let you know when they do.

If you stop meeting any one of these requirements at any time, you won’t be able to earn eBucks with your Premier Account(s) As soon as you fulﬁll the relevant requirement again, you will earn eBucks when you use your account(s) again.

If you hold a Premier Cheque Card and Premier Credit Card on the bundled pricing option, you will no longer have to pay the Monthly Credit Card Rewards Fee on your Premier Credit Card to earn eBucks.

If you only have a Premier Credit Card, i.e. no Premier Cheque Account, you can link your Premier Credit Card to your eBucks account by calling us on 087 320 3200 or visiting www.eBucks.com. A Monthly Credit Card Rewards Fee of R16.50 per month is charged to link your Premier Credit Card to earn eBucks. The R16.50 Monthly Credit Card Rewards Fee is billed monthly to your Premier Credit Card.
