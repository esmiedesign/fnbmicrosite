Automatic Debt Protection

As an FNB Credit Card holder you receive FREE Automatic Debt Protection on the outstanding debt of your credit card in the event of your Death or Permanent Disability

  • Be excused from paying the outstanding balance on your credit card or the specified maximum amount set by the bank, whichever is lesser at the time of your death or permanent disability

FREE Automatic Debt Protection covers you for the amount specified below:

Top-up Debt Protection Plan

We offer a Top-up Debt Protection Plan (TDPP) as a top-up to Automatic Debt Protection to extend your speciﬁed maximum debt cover at an additional cost.

  • Top up your protection plan and extend your maximum debt cover at a fee using the Top-up Debt Protection facility
  • With Top-Up Debt Protection, not only do you get cover in the event of Death or Permanent Disability, you also get cover in the event of Retrenchment as well as Temporary Disability
