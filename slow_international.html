SLOW International Lounge

SLOW International Lounges can be found at the International Terminal of OR Tambo International Airport.

Benefits:
  • Tasty snacks freshly prepared by on-site chefs
  • Freshly brewed coffees, selected wine-tasting and bar service
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi access & power throughout the lounges
  • Private working areas and a meeting room
  • Apple iMacs and printing facilities
  • Individual washrooms and shower facilities
  • A library filled with a wide range of books for your reading pleasure
  • A private spa, offering a complimentary 15-20 minute treatment before your flight
  • A VIP private suite with an en-suite bathroom for families travelling together

Operating Hours
Monday - Friday: 05h00-21h00 | Saturday and Sunday: 07h00- 21h00
