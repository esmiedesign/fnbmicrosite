Comprehensive Global Travel Insurance

This innovative service gives travel ticket holders Comprehensive Global Travel Insurance, both locally and internationally. There's no need to buy additional top-up cover, saving you around R446 per ticket. It includes up to R10 million for medical expenses and R2.5m for personal liability cover, as well as providing cover against personal accidents, cancellation, travel delay, lost and stolen or damaged luggage.

By purchasing the departure and return public conveyance travel tickets in South Africa (or other specified countries as detailed in the policy) at full fare on your Premier Credit Card, prior to the departure, ticket holders are automatically covered for their first 90 days of the journey. This offer only applies if you are a South African citizen (or a citizen of other specified countries as detailed in the policy) up to and including the age of 70.

Please obtain a copy of the Travel Policy prior to travelling and ensure you fully understand the policy. The Travel Policy is obtainable from www.fnb.co.za and from the Comprehensive Global Travel Insurance Help Desk. If anything is unclear, please contact the Comprehensive Global Travel Insurance Help Desk on 0861 490 100.

If you are between 70 and 85, we can offer you another travel insurance option.
