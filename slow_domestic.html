  • Private marble washrooms

  • A library filled with recreational and business books for your reading pleasure

SLOW Lounge Access Rules
Peak Times Include:

Mon to Fri: 04h45-07h00 and 13h00-17h30
Sunday: 15h00-17h30
All other times not specified above are deemed Off-Peak times. Peak and Off-Peak times are applicable to SLOW Domestic Lounges only.

Access Rules:
  • All qualifying cheque and credit cardholders who have access to SLOW Domestic Lounges will only be allowed to enter 90 minutes before their boarding time.
  • Should the lounge capacity be reached, FNB Business Black, FNB Private Wealth and RMB Private Bank customers will be granted preferential access.
  • All entries into the SLOW Lounges are non-transferable. Any additional entries (in excess of those mentioned above) will be charged for. Unused free visits do not roll over to the next month.
  • SLOW Lounge will not accept any cardholders with expired cards.
  • SLOW Lounge will only permit access on presentation and swiping of applicable cards.
  • SLOW Lounge will only permit the cardholder access to the Lounge if the card presented is in their individual name.
  • Only one card will be permitted per cardholder and must be accompanied by a boarding pass. Both the card and the boarding pass must be issued in the same individual's name.
  • SLOW Lounge reserves the right to decline entry should any Lounge be full. Lounge access is subject to availability, according to capacity.
  • SLOW Lounge retains the right to deny access should they deem necessary.
  • Linked (secondary) cheque and credit cards must be in the name of the cardholder.
  • Children younger than 2 years old may access the SLOW Lounge for free.
  • Children older than 2 years old may access SLOW Domestic Lounges as per cardholder access criteria and fees.
  • No children under the age of 18, will be allowed into the lounge without adult supervision.
  • SLOW Domestic Lounges can be found in the domestic terminals of OR Tambo, Cape Town and King Shaka Airports.
