SLOW in the City

Time and Space for extremely busy people

SLOW in the City can be found directly opposite the Sandton Gautrain Station, corner of Rivonia and West street.

Benefits:
  • A range of meeting rooms and workspaces including private lounges, boardrooms, function rooms, signature meeting spaces, cocoons, library desks and private studios
  • Super fast Wi-Fi and 3G
  • Newspapers, magazines and business journals
  • PC workstations and printing facilities
  • A video conferencing suite
  • Power sockets throughout the facility
  • Private washrooms with full shower facilities
  • A cloakroom for luggage and coats, and personal lockers
  • A bar offering specialty teas and coffees, wine and cocktails
  • A bistro serving breakfast, lunch and afternoon teas
  • Self Service Flight Check-in
  • A library with the latest books from Exclus1ves.co.za
Operating Hours
Monday - Friday: 07h00 - 19h00

What will it cost?
SLOW in the City is a charged-for facility. Only card payments are accepted as SLOW in the City operates as a cash-less facility.

SLOW in the City provides three access options for eligible FNB Cardholders: Day Access, a 10 day Monthly Membership and a 20 day Monthly Membership.

Membership package also includes:
  • 25% Discount on breakfast and lunch buffet
  • 10% Discount to exclusive SLOW Agenda events
  • Lounge facilities
  • Free meeting room reservations for up to 4 hours per day
  • Unreserved meeting rooms, library desks and private study quarters
  • Use of the Light Room and Dark Room facilities
  • Wi-Fi, Internet stations
  • Shower and cloakroom facilities
  • Reciprocal access to the Hospital Club in SOHO, London
  • 10 Complimentary black and white prints per visit
