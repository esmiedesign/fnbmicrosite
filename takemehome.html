Take Me Home

Whether you are planning a special night out or don’t want to drive alone - you don’t have to drive. As an FNB Premier Credit Card holder with a linked or Standalone Petro Card, we’ve added a free Take Me Home benefit that you can utilise six times per year.

Operated by Europ Assistance, this service will ensure that a vehicle is dispatched to your location with two drivers equipped with a cellphone and GPS. They will collect you at the agreed time, with one of them driving you home in your car, while the other will follow behind as back-up and provide transport to the primary driver on completion of the trip.

A little planning is required, so please remember to book this service at least two hours before you need it during peak periods (Thursday evening to Sunday morning) and one hour before the driver is required in non-peak periods (Sunday evening to Thursday morning).

Process
To make a booking contact Take Me Home on 0861 643 278
  1. Provide your linked Petro Card number
  2. Specify your pick-up point and collection time
  3. Specify your destination drop-off point
  4. Provide a primary and secondary contact number
  5. Provide the registration number, make and colour of your vehicle
  6. Once a pick up point has been arranged, Take Me Home will notify you when your driver has arrived. You have 15 minutes to meet your designated driver and begin your trip home
