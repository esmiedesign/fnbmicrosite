inContact and Decline inContact

inContact  is an innovative messaging system that keeps you updated on your account balances, helping you manage your money and prevent fraud on your account. The inContact  service sends you a message via SMS or email each time a transaction (above R100) is carried out on any of your FNB accounts at no extra charge.

Decline inContact  puts the power back in your hands, by offering corrective action for a declined transaction and helping you understand why it happened. Sometimes transactions do not go through as a result of a problem with the terminal and you can be left stranded when the teller advises you that the error is on your credit card. This will not be the case anymore.
