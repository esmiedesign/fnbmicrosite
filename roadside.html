AA Emergency Roadside Assistance*

With a Petro Card from FNB you are automatically entitled to free AA Emergency Roadside Assistance to help you out in case of an emergency.

This service includes:

AA Roadside Repair Services
  • Emergency refuelling when stranded (up to a maximum of 10 litres)
  • Flat battery restarting
  • Changing of flat tyre(s)
  • Key lock-out services

AA Courtesy Services**

AA will provide the following services:
  • Car hire to your destination or home address
  • Overnight accommodation for you and up to four passengers or
  • Transportation or costs of transportation for you to collect your repaired vehicle

Free Tow-In Service
  • In the event of a mechanical or electrical vehicle breakdown (covered up to a maximum of R1 000)
AA Stand By You Service

Request this service when your car breaks down and an armed security guard will stay with you until the AA patrolman arrives.

Process

To make use of AA Emergency Roadside Assistance all you need is your Petro Card and simply do the following:
  • Call 0860 70 60 50 and provide the operator with your Petro Card number (16 digit number printed on the card)
  • An AA appointed contractor will be dispatched to provide you with the required assistance
  • On arrival the AA appointed contractor will ask you to produce your Petro Card and proof of identity in order to facilitate the emergency assistance

In the event of an accident

If you are involved in an accident, the AA will arrange for your vehicle to be towed to the nearest AA approved repair centre or destination of your choice. You will however be responsible for the costs incurred.
